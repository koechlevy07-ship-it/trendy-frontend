Shipping Information

We offer free delivery on all orders over Ksh 15,000 within Nairobi. Standard delivery within Nairobi is Ksh 500 and takes 1-3 business days. Upcountry deliveries take 2-5 business days at a flat rate of Ksh 800.

International shipping is available on select items. Please contact us for a quote.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team via WhatsApp at +254 728 985 417 or email markelpalace@gmail.com.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the item is defective or incorrect.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and WhatsApp. You can track your order status in your account dashboard or contact our support team.

Care Instructions

To ensure the longevity of your Trendy Wardrobe garments, please follow these care guidelines:

  • Dry Clean: Most of our luxury items are best dry cleaned to preserve fabric quality and color.
  • Gentle Wash: For machine-washable items, use cold water on a gentle cycle. Turn garments inside out.
  • No Bleach: Do not use chlorine bleach or harsh detergents.
  • Ironing: Iron on low to medium heat. Avoid direct heat on prints or embellishments.
  • Storage: Hang on padded hangers. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Leather/Suede: Professional cleaning only. Store with proper ventilation.

Always refer to the care label inside your garment for specific instructions.
