Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Trendy Wardrobe, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Trendy Wardrobe, we collect information that you provide directly and information generated through your use of our services:

  • Personal details: Your full name, email address, and phone number when you create an account or place an order.
  • Shipping information: Your delivery address and associated location details required to fulfill your orders.
  • Payment information: We currently operate on a Cash on Delivery (COD) model. Should we introduce online payment methods in the future, payment details will be collected through secure, encrypted payment processors.
  • Browsing behavior: Pages visited, products viewed, search queries, and interaction patterns on our website, collected through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process, fulfill, and deliver your orders accurately and on time.
  • To communicate with you regarding order confirmations, delivery updates, and customer support inquiries.
  • To improve our website, product offerings, and overall shopping experience based on your feedback and browsing patterns.
  • To send you marketing communications about new arrivals, promotions, and exclusive offers — only when you have given explicit consent.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraudulent activities and unauthorized access to our platform.

3. Information Sharing

Your trust is important to us. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Delivery partners: We share your name, phone number, and shipping address with our trusted delivery partners solely for the purpose of fulfilling and delivering your orders.
  • Payment processors: When online payment options become available, your payment information will be handled by certified, PCI-compliant payment processors.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Trendy Wardrobe, our customers, or others.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • All account authentication is secured using JSON Web Tokens (JWT) to ensure safe and verified access.
  • Your data is stored on secure servers with appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.
  • We regularly review our security practices to ensure they remain current and effective.

While we strive to protect your personal information using commercially acceptable means, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We are committed to promptly addressing any security concerns that arise.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for core site functionality, including maintaining your shopping cart, keeping you logged in, and processing your orders. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website, helping us identify areas for improvement and optimize performance. These cookies collect aggregated, anonymized data.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.
  • Marketing opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by contacting us or using the unsubscribe link in our emails.

To exercise any of these rights, please reach out to us via WhatsApp or email. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Order data: Retained for 2 years from the date of your last order to support customer service inquiries, returns, and record-keeping.
  • Account data: Retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request account and data deletion at any time by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Trendy Wardrobe's services are not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us right away.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or operational needs. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
